Overview

Purpose

The Primary goal of an online presence for my rafting business is to boost visibility, attract more customers, and enrich information access, which in turn increase bookings and grows revenue.

Audience

My target Audience are people of ages 12 to 60 years with an average financial background. This is to say I would provide an affordable services where the individuals or target audience could have their needs met. Also, to the Outdoor enthusiactical people, this site is meant for them to explore and to know much information regarding the rafting activities around the world that other sites might not provide. Most people could not afford some servives of some rafting companies and hence aren't getting their needs met but with Tech's Rafting, everything is made affordable so the Targetred audience could have their needs met. This site could be accessible on a destop, a laptop and even on a smart mobile phone.
